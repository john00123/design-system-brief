Theming / Dark-mode

We have been working for the last couple of months to introduce theming to our product portfolio starting with Prism.

We are happy to be making significant progress, targeting our next major release (6.0) to introduce this capability to our customers. This project has been a collaboration between our engineering and design teams across products and geos.

CContaining a set of Key Screen templates and components, this kit is designed to accelerate iterations and approvals during the conception phase. We like to think of this not as an additional step in the work process, but an option to focus on workflows and meeting requirements instead of discussion revolving around aesthetics.
