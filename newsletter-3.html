There's a new kit in town

While we're incredibly proud of you engement and positive response to our 2.5 release (***), we have also been busy working on the next step towards a simpler, yet more versatile desing system.

Today we are excited to announce another milestons to the Nutanix design system: Meet our new Wireframe Kit!

Containing a set of Key Screen templates and components, this kit is designed to accelerate iterations and approvals during the conception phase. We like to think of this not as an additional step in teh work process, but an option to focus on wokflows and meeting requirements instead of discussion revolving around aesthetics.
